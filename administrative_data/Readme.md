# Maximizing the use of, and value derived from, linked and integrated administrative data assets to inform government policy decision-making, Canberra, May 2026

*Title*: Data access in Australia: a cynical statistician's perspective


Goals of the day:

* Overview of national data assets and their policy applications

* Academic research presentations on key policy areas (e.g. NDIS, mental health, Indigenous health, and data-driven case studies)

* Discussion on enabling future research through funding, capacity building, and institutional collaboration

* Designing future research to inform current and emerging policy challenges
