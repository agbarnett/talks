Ethical guidance on study designs

  • National statement: “Research that has merit is: […] designed or developed using methods appropriate for achieving the aims of the proposal.”

  • Declaration of Helsinki: “Medical research involving human participants must have a scientifically sound and rigorous design and execution that are likely to produce reliable, valid, and valuable knowledge and avoid research waste.”

  • Should committees cover study design? Each committee post a public statement
