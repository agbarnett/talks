Gold OA

Abandon

Incentivizes the mass production and publication of low-quality science, corrupting the scientific process

Institutional APC waivers

Treat with caution

Unlikely to reward high-quality journals over low-quality journals. Likely unavailable to researchers at underfunded institutions

Hybrid OA

Abandon

Likely subject to the same incentives as Gold OA

Green/Diamond OA

Recommend with reservations

They plausibly create good incentives, but may struggle to be sustained without external support.

Society journals

Recommend with reservations

Appropriate policies are unclear in detail. Societies need safeguards against corruption and abuse.

Formal theory of journal policies

Recommend

Ideal system

  • Free for researchers and the public to read

  • Universal submission system

  • Easy post-publication peer review (see PubPeer)

  • Links to code, data and other additional material

  • Quality rated by individual article rather than the journal
